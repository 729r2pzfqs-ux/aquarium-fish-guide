About FishFinder

Helping aquarium hobbyists find the perfect fish since 2026

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a thriving aquarium — without the guesswork. FishFinder was built to make fishkeeping accessible, helping beginners avoid common mistakes and experienced hobbyists discover new species.

Too many people give up on aquariums because they bought incompatible fish, chose the wrong species for their tank size, or didn't know about cycling. We're here to change that.

Our Approach

Every piece of information on FishFinder is researched and fact-checked. We combine scientific data with real-world experience from the fishkeeping community.

We focus on practical advice that actually works — not outdated rules of thumb. Our recommendations prioritize fish welfare and long-term success over quick fixes.
